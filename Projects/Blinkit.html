Introduction to Blinkit

Blinkit is a leading retail and grocery delivery service that focuses on providing customers with fast and convenient shopping experiences...

Business Requirement

The objective of the Blinkit Dashboard was to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's operations...

Key Objectives

  • Analyze sales trends and their impact on customer satisfaction.
  • Explore the correlation between inventory metrics and outlet performance.
  • Provide actionable insights to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

KPIs Included

  • Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
  • Average Sales: The average revenue per sale.
  • Number of Items: The total count of different items sold.
  • Average Rating: The average customer rating for items sold.

Visualizations & Insights

  • Total Sales by Fat Content: Donut Chart.
  • Total Sales by Item Type: Bar Chart.
  • Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales: Stacked Column Chart.
  • Total Sales by Outlet Establishment: Line Chart.
  • Sales by Outlet Size: Donut/Pie Chart.
  • Sales by Outlet Location: Funnel Map.
  • All Metrics by Outlet Type: Matrix Card.

Technologies Used

  • Power BI: For creating the interactive dashboard and visualizations.
  • Data Modeling: To create relationships and calculate KPIs.
  • Data Analysis Expressions (DAX): For deriving insights from complex metrics.

Outcomes

The Blinkit Dashboard serves as a valuable tool for data-driven decision-making. It empowers stakeholders to:

  • Identify sales trends and growth opportunities.
  • Improve inventory distribution across outlets.
  • Tailor strategies to enhance customer satisfaction.
